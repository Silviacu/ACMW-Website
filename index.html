Who We Are

We are an organization that focuses on bringing awareness of women in the IT/Computing industry.


Community

We strive to create a community for women in technology where they can come together to learn from each other, bond, network, discuss ideas, and encourage each other.

Awareness

We want women in tech to be aware of all the opportunities they have and to encourage others in the community to persue these opportunities. Some of our opportunities include Women In tech Conferences, Female Hackathons, Girls in tech Community service opportunities and more.

Empowerment

We encourage women to always reach for more and to never give up! To improve our abilities as leaders and successful women in technology. Some leadership meetings we held were: workshops to improve resumes, interviews, essay writing, job fair confidence, and hackathon teamwork.

Mentorship

We give students opportunities to find mentors and/or provide mentorship to girls in our clubs as well as in our community. We are always willing to hold each other up and achieve the best.
