About us

We are Temple University’s ACM-W chapter.

Our organization’s purpose is to open the doors of technology to women of all ages. We are here to help women in the field succeed and to increase awareness of the woman's role in technological fields with emphasis on computing and machinery.

This year we are focusing on building a community in technology at Temple University. Through homework nights, mentorship opportunities, workshops, discussions, networking events, career panels and team activities we hope to create meaningful connections with other women in our department and bring understanding about different careers in technology.
